# Pizza Sales Performance Dashboard
## Project Overview

This project features an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard designed to analyze pizza sales, delivery performance, customer ordering patterns, and operational efficiency across multiple restaurants. By transforming raw operational data into clear visual KPIs, the dashboard identifies delivery bottlenecks, peak ordering periods, and key performance trends to support faster deliveries and data-driven business decisions.

## Key Business Metrics (KPIs)
**Total Orders:** 1004

**Average Delivery Duration:** 29 Minutes

**Average Delivery Efficiency:** 6.40km

**Average Delay Minute:** 17.62

## Deep-Dive Insights 
1. Order Performance & Customer Behavior
* Peak Ordering Hours: Evening hours recorded the highest order volume, making dinner time the busiest period and indicating when staffing and delivery resources should be prioritized.

* Card Payment Preference: Card emerged as the most preferred payment method, indicating a strong customer preference for seamless and convinient digital transactions. 


2. Delivery Performance & Operational Insights

* Domino’s Delivery Delays: Domino’s recorded the highest average delivery time among all brands, revealing an operational bottleneck that could negatively impact customer satisfaction.

* Delivery Efficiency: Orders were delivered in an average of 29 minutes, indicating generally efficient operations while highlighting opportunities to reduce delays during peak periods.

## Strategic Recommendations
* Optimize Delivery Routes: Streamline delivery routes during peak traffic periods to minimize delays and improve delivery efficiency.

* Increase Peak-Hour Capacity: Expand staffing and delivery capacity between 6:00 PM and 8:00 PM to effectively meet the highest customer demand.

* Improve Domino’s Operations: Investigate the root causes of Domino’s delivery delays and implement operational improvements to enhance service performance.

* Promote Digital Payments: Continue encouraging card and other digital payment options to improve customer convenience and transaction efficiency.

* Refine Delivery Time Estimates: Incorporate order size into delivery time estimates to provide more accurate delivery expectations and improve customer satisfaction.
